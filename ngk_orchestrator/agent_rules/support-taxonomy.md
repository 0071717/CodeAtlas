# Support Taxonomy

ngk only promotes findings that are:

1. schema-valid,
2. in scope,
3. supported by existing Atlas fact IDs,
4. backed by non-stale evidence,
5. not contradicted by another audited result.

`inferred`, `not_confirmed`, `contradicted`, and `out_of_scope` items are useful, but they remain warnings, uncertainty, conflicts, or rejected findings.

A synthesis result must never increase confidence beyond its weakest supporting finding.
