# Citation Rules

Every architectural, API, UI-flow, data-flow, model, test, contract, security, or business-rule claim must use a support label.

Support labels:

- `supported`: the supplied Atlas facts directly support the claim. Include fact IDs.
- `inferred`: the claim is plausible but not proven. Include a reason.
- `not_confirmed`: the supplied Atlas context does not prove the claim.
- `contradicted`: supplied evidence conflicts.
- `out_of_scope`: outside the assigned agent role.

Do not cite a fact unless it supports the exact claim. Do not promote inferred or not-confirmed claims into accepted findings.
